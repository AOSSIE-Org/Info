# GSoC-Summit

If you have been selected to represent AOSSIE in the GSoC Summit, please follow the steps below:

1. Start the procedure to obtain a visa as soon as possible.
   Google will eventually give you an invitation letter that you will need to show
   to the consulate to apply for the visa. But don't wait. Get prepared even before receiving this letter.
   Considering scheduling your appointment even before receiving the invitation letter,
   but on a date when you know you will have received the letter already.
2. Prepare slides of your 3-minute presentation at the summit.
3. Once your visa has been approved (and only after visa approval), search for flights.
   Please look for reasonable options in terms of price.
   Show the flight that you would like to book to AOSSIE's director.
4. Once your desired flight has been approved by AOSSIE's director, book it.
5. Do not book accommodation, because it is provided by Google.
6. Travel to the summit.
7. Use public transportation (train, tram, bus) to go from the airport to Google's provided acommodation
   and to the summit venue.
8. Keep your visa, flight and public transportation receipts.
9. Return home.
10. Prepare a short report about your experience at the summit and how it brought value to AOSSIE.
11. Once Google pays travel stipends to the organization (usually around November),
   provide your receipts to AOSSIE's director so that your expenses are refunded.

The maximum amount `M` that can be refunded is 1200 USD in case AOSSIE sends a single delegate 
or 900 USD per delegate in case AOSSIE sends two delegates. If `E` is the total amount of your expenses, 
then the actual amount that will be refunded is `min(E, M)`.

AOSSIE refunds only expenses that have been incurred with visa, flights and public transportation.

In the case of visa expenses, AOSSIE refunds only the official visa cost, as stated in the official website 
of the destination country's consulate. Any other fee charged by agencies that you choose to 
engage to apply for the visa are not refunded.

If you wish to choose other forms of local transportation (e.g. taxi, ride sharing or renting a car), you may. 
But AOSSIE will only refund an amount at most equal to what you would have spent 
if you had used public transportation.

If you wish, you may arrive earlier or stay longer in the summit's country. In fact, 
you are encouraged to take advantage of the opportunity to get to know the region around the summit, 
to meet personal or professional connections there, and so on. 
But any costs associated with your extended stay are not refunded by AOSSIE.

Refunds depend on the following conditions:
* AOSSIE must have received the travel stipends from Google. 
  This may not occur if we do not complete all midterm and final evaluations.
* The delegate must have presented at the summit.
* The delegate must have prepared the short participation report upon returning.
